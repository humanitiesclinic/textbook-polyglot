Remark 1.4.2. Word Order Typology.

Linguist Joseph Greenberg demonstrated that word order is not random. Languages that place the Object after the Verb (SVO, VSO) tend to have prepositions (in the house), while languages that place the Object before the Verb (SOV) tend to have postpositions (the house in). These correlations suggest deep structural principles organizing human language.
